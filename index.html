project introduction

Appointment Management System

This is a management system. Users can apply to request administrator approval. Users can review responses at any time. Administrators can review history.

Therapist Appointment APP

Patients can see the therapist's basic information through the therapist list and send an appointment request with a time to a certain therapist. Therapists can accept applications and obtain the patient's address. Patients can rate the therapist after the meeting. This review is visible to everyone. Patients and their corresponding therapists can communicate instantly via chat.

Online Shopping Page and Data Management

This is primarily a mysql project. Users can log in to their account and post products. Anyone can review products posted by others. Users can post up to 3 products and 3 comments per day. Products can be sorted and printed based on price or category.
